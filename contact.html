Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and company

What areas do you serve for mining transportation? +

We primarily serve mining operations in and around Johannesburg and the surrounding Gauteng province. However, we can arrange transportation solutions for mining sites throughout South Africa depending on the specific requirements and scale of the operation.

How far in advance should I book beauty services? +

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance for standard beauty services. For special occasions like weddings or major events, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead to ensure availability. We do accommodate last-minute appointments when possible, based on technician availability.

When is the next "Make Heaven Crowded" event? +

The next "Make Heaven Crowded" inspirational event is scheduled for September 2026. This annual gathering typically takes place in September each year. Please check our events page or contact our events team for specific dates, ticket information, and event details as they become available.

Do you offer corporate accounts for transportation services? +

Yes, we offer specialized corporate accounts for mining companies with customized transportation solutions, dedicated account management, detailed reporting, and flexible billing options. Contact our transportation team to discuss setting up a corporate account tailored to your company's specific needs.

What safety measures do you have for transportation services? +

Our transportation services adhere to strict safety protocols including regular vehicle maintenance checks, certified and experienced drivers, comprehensive insurance coverage, GPS tracking, and 24/7 support. All our drivers undergo rigorous background checks and continuous safety training to ensure the highest standards of passenger safety.
